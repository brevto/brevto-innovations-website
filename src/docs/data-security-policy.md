This Data Security Policy describes the measures **Brevto Innovations Private Limited** ("**Brevto**", "**we**", "**us**", "**our**") undertakes to protect information collected through **brevtoinnovations.vercel.app** (the "**Website**"), in line with the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the SPDI Rules, 2011.

---

## 1. Our Commitment to Security

We are committed to protecting the limited personal information we collect (primarily through our Contact Form) using reasonable security practices and procedures appropriate to the nature of data we handle. As a company that does not process payments, store user account credentials, or accept direct file uploads on our own servers, our data security risk surface is intentionally minimized by design.

---

## 2. Hosting & Infrastructure Security

- The Website is hosted on **Vercel**, a cloud infrastructure provider that implements industry-standard security practices, including HTTPS/TLS encryption for data in transit, DDoS protection, and secure deployment pipelines.
- Backend services, where used, are supported by **Firebase** (Google), which provides enterprise-grade infrastructure security, including encryption at rest and in transit.
- Communications are managed through **Google Workspace and Gmail**, which maintain industry-standard security certifications and practices.

---

## 3. Data Transmission

All data submitted through the Website (including via the Contact Form) is transmitted using **HTTPS/TLS encryption**, helping protect information from interception during transmission.

---

## 4. Data Minimization

We follow a data minimization approach:
- We collect only the information necessary for the stated purpose (Name, Email, Subject, Message via the Contact Form)
- We do not store payment information, as we do not process payments
- We do not maintain user account databases, as the Website does not support account creation
- We do not host file uploads directly; resume/application files are submitted via external Google Forms, governed by Google's own security infrastructure

---

## 5. Access Control

Access to any information submitted through the Website is restricted to authorized personnel of Brevto Innovations Private Limited on a need-to-know basis, for the purpose of responding to inquiries or managing business operations.

---

## 6. Third-Party Security

We rely on reputable third-party service providers — **Vercel, Google Workspace, Gmail, Firebase, Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager, and Vercel Analytics** — each of which maintains its own security standards and certifications. While we select providers with strong security practices, we cannot guarantee the security practices of third parties and encourage you to review their respective security and privacy documentation.

---

## 7. Data Breach Response

In the unlikely event of a data security breach affecting personal information collected by us, we will take reasonable steps to:
- Investigate and contain the breach promptly
- Assess the scope and impact of the breach
- Notify affected individuals and relevant authorities where required under applicable Indian law
- Take corrective action to prevent recurrence

---

## 8. No Guarantee of Absolute Security

While we implement reasonable security measures, **no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure**. We cannot guarantee absolute security of information transmitted to or stored by us or our third-party service providers, and any transmission is at your own risk.

---

## 9. Your Role in Security

We encourage users to:
- Avoid submitting sensitive or confidential information through the Contact Form
- Exercise caution when submitting information via linked third-party Google Forms
- Report any suspected security vulnerabilities or concerns to us promptly

---

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Data Security Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
